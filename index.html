That Techy Guy

Because IT isn't about scripts and codes - It's about helping people.

Remember that time, just before the big meeting, when your printer suddenly stopped working? Or that time when the internet cut out, for likely no reason other than pure spite? Remember how you shouted down the hall for "that techy guy" to come have a look at it?

That guy is me.

Programming is my passion, but I understand better than anyone that it probably isn't yours. You don't want to know why making the header of your site bigger broke the clock, or how one might go about adding a new feature, you want everything to just work, so you can go back to doing the things you are passionate about.

I have been programming since I was 13. I started out with languages like Java, C++ and C#, then a few years ago made the move to web development, learning Javascript, HTML and CSS. I now work regularly with PHP and NodeJS.
